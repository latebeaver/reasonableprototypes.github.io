Hi! My name is Noel Chung (sometimes El No). I study sociology of technology.



With a background in sociology and media studies, I am fascinated by how (digital) technology forms social practices and norms which in turn inform technological designs. Currently, I am studying social and spatial practices emerging through the rise of digital platforms that facilitate movements of people, things, and data.

Throughout my career, I took consultant and manager roles in strategy, analytics, and product development working with people with a variety of professional and cultural background including data scientists, designers, aid workers and sales managers (See my CV in PDF).

So far I've spent 60% my life in Korea, 21% in the UK, 16% in China and 3% in Kenya.

Outside work, I love looking after my plants, seeing plays and playing tennis.
